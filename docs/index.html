What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin was the first cryptocurrency created; Satoshi Nakamoto created it in 2009. It serves to exchange goods and services like the Euro or the American Dollar, but with the advantage of being an unregulated electronic currency, possessing novel features such as safety, efficiency, and transaction easiness. Through Bitcoin, users can make transactions directly to the recipient, without intermediaries, since they are verified by an accounting system called blockchain

What is Blockchain?

Also known as the Accounting Book, blockchain is a database distributed in blocks, where each block has a verifiable registry of all transactions that were made in its history; the information contained is permanent and unchangeable, thus this systems is considered as the most secure system ever created

What is Cryptocurrency mining?

Cryptocurrency mining is the resolution of mathematical problems through specialized equipment and software to efficiently issue a certain number of bitcoins in return. Cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin are generated using algorithms to manage and verify transactions

What is Cloudmining?

What are Cryptocurrencies

Cryptocurrencies

These are virtual or digital currencies used to exchange goods and services through an electronic transaction system, without the need of an intermediary. That is to say the aforesaid system is decentralized. It is carried out through cryptography to assure all transactions and control the creation of new coins.
