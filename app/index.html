What are Cryptocurrencies

Cryptocurrencies

These are virtual or digital currencies used to exchange goods and services through an electronic transaction system, without the need of an intermediary. That is to say the aforesaid system is decentralized. It is carried out through cryptography to assure all transactions and control the creation of new coins.
